## Research Interests

My research sits at the intersection of **Embodied AI**, **Dexterous Manipulation**, and **Human-Robot Interaction**. I'm particularly interested in:

- **Teleoperation & Retargeting** — Mapping human hand motions to dexterous robot hands in real-time using task-space vector optimization and IK solvers
- **Visuomotor Policy Learning** — Diffusion-based policies for multi-modal action distributions in manipulation tasks
- **Humanoid Robotics** — Whole-body loco-manipulation, locomotion control, and sim-to-real transfer
- **World Models** — Using predictive models for planning and data augmentation in robotic control

## What I'm Working On
